Welcome!

I am an assistant professor in Environmental Studies and Biology at the University of Oregon. Please contact me to learn more about opportunities for graduate students and postdocs. hallett (at) uoregon (dot) edu

Research in the Hallett Lab focuses on plant community ecology and restoration ecology. Our goal is to produce "usable" science to improve ecosystem management. We use a combination of long-term data analysis, population modeling and field experiments to this end. Our work spans a variety of systems, including working rangelands, serpentine grasslands, woodlands and alpine.
