Welcome!

I will be starting as an assistant professor in Environmental Studies and Biology at the University of Oregon in Sept 2017. Please contact me to learn more about opportunities for graduate students and postdocs. lauren (dot) hallett (at) colorado (dot) edu

Currently I am research associate at the University of Colorado Boulder, specializing in plant community ecology and restoration ecology. My goal is to produce "usable" science to improve ecosystem management. I use a combination of long-term data analysis, population modeling and field experiments to this end. My research spans a variety of systems, including working rangelands, serpentine grasslands, woodlands and alpine.
